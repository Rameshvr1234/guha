FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries about our projects and processes

What types of properties does GUHA Promoters develop?

GUHA Promoters specializes in premium residential villa developments. We focus on 2, 3, and 4 BHK luxury villas with modern amenities, located in prime areas of Coimbatore. Each project is designed with quality construction, contemporary architecture, and sustainable living features.

How can I schedule a site visit?

You can schedule a site visit by calling us at +91-9366624545, emailing info@guhapromoters.com, or filling out the contact form on this page. We recommend booking in advance to ensure personalized attention during your visit. Our team will coordinate a convenient time and provide you with detailed project information.

What is the typical timeline for project completion?

Our residential projects typically take 18-24 months from construction commencement to handover. GUHA Promoters has a proven track record of 100% on-time delivery. We provide regular construction updates and maintain transparent communication throughout the development process.

Are financing options available?

Yes, we have tie-ups with leading banks and financial institutions to facilitate home loan approvals. Our team can guide you through the financing process and connect you with our banking partners for competitive interest rates and flexible payment plans.

What amenities are included in your projects?

Our projects feature comprehensive amenities including swimming pools, clubhouses, fitness centers, landscaped gardens, children's play areas, 24/7 security with CCTV surveillance, power backup, rainwater harvesting, and covered parking. Specific amenities vary by project - please contact us for detailed information about a particular development.
