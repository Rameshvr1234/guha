About GUHA Elite

GUHA Elite represents the pinnacle of luxury living in the city's most prestigious North Business District. This ultra-premium project offers spacious 3 & 4 BHK apartments and exclusive penthouses designed for discerning homeowners who demand the finest.

Spanning 4.5 acres of prime real estate, GUHA Elite features only 96 exclusive residences across 15 floors, ensuring privacy and exclusivity. Each residence is meticulously crafted with imported materials, smart home automation, and breathtaking city views.

Launching in September 2025, this is a rare opportunity to own a piece of architectural excellence. With resort-style amenities, concierge services, and proximity to premium business hubs, GUHA Elite is where your success story begins.
