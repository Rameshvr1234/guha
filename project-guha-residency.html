About GUHA Residency

GUHA Residency is a premium residential project that redefines luxury living in the city. Spread across 3.5 acres, this thoughtfully designed development offers spacious 2, 3 & 4 BHK apartments with modern amenities and stunning architecture.

Located in the prime area of the city, GUHA Residency provides excellent connectivity to major IT hubs, schools, hospitals, and shopping centers. The project features 180 elegantly designed apartments across 12 floors, ensuring privacy and exclusivity for residents.

With 67% construction completed, we're on track for timely delivery in December 2025. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction ensures that every aspect of your dream home exceeds expectations.
